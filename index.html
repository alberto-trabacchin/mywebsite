Something about me

I earned a bachelor's degree in Mechatronics Engineering in 2021 at the University of Padova, where I am currently attending a Master's course in the same field. Since October 2022 I joined a Double Degree Program organized by the T.I.M.E. Association between the University of Padova and the Yokohama National University. Thus, in 2024, I am going to get both the Italian Master's degree in Mechatronics Engineering and the Japanese Master's degree in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. I am currently doing a research in the Fujimoto Laboratory, in collaboration with Magna, related to some improvements for ADAS systems. This project involves driver-behavior analysis for pedestrians-collision avoidance.
